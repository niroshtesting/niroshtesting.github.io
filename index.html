About : The Conversation

Conversation with leaders is a live online professional discussion event organized by Kumaraguru College of Liberal Arts and Science, Kumaraguru Institutions, Coimbatore, India to hear and learn from a panel of speakers from around the globe as they share insights and perspectives on winning strategies to help the diverse virtual audience not just to see through the current crisis but also foresee and build a wonderful future.

The virtual webcast will bring together leaders, businesses, professionals, industry experts, entrepreneurs, policy makers, social workers, government officials and influencers to discuss important topics of business, finance, health and society. The experts from across industries and regions will not only share their experiences, best practices but also talk about the new opportunities, changes and disruptions that will emerge from the rapidly evolving global economy, key priorities and the future directions.
